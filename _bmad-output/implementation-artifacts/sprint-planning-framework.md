## üìã EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This framework breaks down the 4 implementation phases (Earthworks, Infrastructure, Planting, Animals) into manageable 2-week sprints with clear deliverables, resource requirements, and family coordination needs.

**Key Principles:**
- 2-week sprint cycles for predictable rhythm
- Seasonal alignment (monsoon, winter, summer strategies)
- Family labor coordination (rotation system)
- Budget tracking per sprint
- Weather contingency planning

---

## üéØ SPRINT STRUCTURE

### Sprint Components
Each sprint includes:
1. **Goals**: Clear deliverables
2. **Duration**: 2 weeks (10 working days)
3. **Team**: Family assignments + contractor needs
4. **Budget**: Estimated costs
5. **Prerequisites**: What must be complete first
6. **Deliverables**: Measurable outcomes
7. **Risks**: Potential blockers
8. **Quality Checks**: Acceptance criteria

### Sprint Rhythm
```
Week 1-2: Sprint execution
Weekend: Sprint review & planning next sprint
Monday: Next sprint kickoff
```

## üå¶Ô∏è SEASONAL ADAPTATIONS

### Monsoon Season (July-September)
- **Planting:** Maximum tree planting (natural irrigation)
- **Earthworks:** Pause excavation (mud issues)
- **Construction:** Focus on roofing, avoid masonry
- **Strategy:** Front-load planting sprints, back-load infrastructure

### Winter Season (October-February)
- **Planting:** Kitchen gardens, cool-season crops
- **Construction:** Ideal for masonry, concrete work
- **Animals:** Introduce chickens/ducks (cooler stress)
- **Strategy:** Heavy infrastructure work

### Summer Season (March-June)
- **Earthworks:** Prime time for excavation (dry soil)
- **Planting:** Pre-monsoon tree prep (pit digging)
- **Irrigation:** Install systems before monsoon
- **Strategy:** Front-load earthworks and irrigation

## üö® RISK MANAGEMENT

### Common Sprint Blockers

**1. Weather Delays**
- **Risk:** Monsoon extends, winter construction impossible
- **Mitigation:** 2-week buffer in schedule, alternate indoor tasks

**2. Contractor No-Shows**
- **Risk:** Excavator/mason unavailable
- **Mitigation:** Book 4-6 weeks in advance, backup vendors

**3. Budget Overruns**
- **Risk:** Material costs spike mid-project
- **Mitigation:** Purchase critical materials upfront, 15% contingency

**4. Family Labor Shortage**
- **Risk:** Illness, emergency, burnout
- **Mitigation:** 3-family minimum rule (1 family off per sprint OK)

**5. Supply Chain Issues**
- **Risk:** Trees, materials unavailable
- **Mitigation:** Order 1 sprint ahead, local suppliers prioritized
