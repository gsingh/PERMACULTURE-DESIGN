#### 3. Simple Flow Rate Test (If Possible)

**Method 1: Bucket Test (Most Accurate)**
1. Get a large bucket (10-20 liter capacity, measure volume)
2. When pump is running, fill bucket from outlet
3. Time how long to fill (use phone timer)
4. Calculate: **Liters per minute (LPM)** = Bucket volume / Time in minutes
5. Convert to GPM: LPM √∑ 3.785 = GPM

**Example:**
- 20 liter bucket fills in 30 seconds (0.5 minutes)
- 20 / 0.5 = 40 LPM
- 40 / 3.785 = 10.6 GPM

**Record:**
- [ ] Bucket volume: _____ liters
- [ ] Fill time: _____ seconds
- [ ] **Flow rate: _____ LPM / _____ GPM**

**Method 2: Hose Length Method (Rough Estimate)**
- Measure how far water sprays from outlet (feet)
- Strong flow = 15-20 ft spray = 15-25 GPM (5 HP pump typical)
- Medium flow = 10-15 ft spray = 10-15 GPM (3 HP pump typical)
- Weak flow = 5-10 ft spray = 5-10 GPM (undersized or old pump)

**Why Critical:** Tells us if pump can meet your permaculture needs (target: 20-30 GPM for 4 families)
