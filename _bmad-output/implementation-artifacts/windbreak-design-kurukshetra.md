## ğŸŒ± PLANTING PROTOCOL

### **PHASE 1: Site Preparation (March-April)**

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Mark planting positions with stakes and lime powder
- [ ] Dig pits: 
  - Row 1: 3ft x 3ft x 3ft (large trees)
  - Row 2: 2ft x 2ft x 2ft (medium)
  - Row 3: 1.5ft x 1.5ft x 1.5ft (shrubs)
- [ ] Fill pits with mix:
  - 50% original soil
  - 30% compost/FYM
  - 20% sand (if soil is clay-heavy)
  - Add 200g rock phosphate per pit (Row 1 & 2)

### **PHASE 2: Planting (June-July with Monsoon)**

**Why Monsoon is Critical:**
- Natural rainfall = less irrigation labor
- High humidity = better establishment
- Cooler temperatures = less transplant shock
- Extended growing season before winter

**Planting Steps:**
1. **2-3 days before monsoon:** Plant taller trees (Row 1)
2. **With first good rain:** Plant Row 2 & 3
3. **Immediate watering:** Even in monsoon, water after planting
4. **Mulch heavily:** 4-6 inch layer around each plant
5. **Stake tall saplings:** Bamboo stakes for Row 1 trees

### **PHASE 3: Establishment Care (Year 1-2)**

**Irrigation Schedule:**

**Year 1:**
- Monsoon months (July-Sept): Natural rain, supplement if 7+ days dry
- Post-monsoon (Oct-Jan): Weekly watering
- Summer (Feb-June): 2-3 times per week (critical!)

**Year 2:**
- Monsoon: Minimal/no irrigation
- Post-monsoon: Bi-weekly
- Summer: Weekly

**Year 3+:** Most trees self-sufficient except extreme drought

**Maintenance Tasks:**
- **Weeding:** Monthly around base (first 2 years)
- **Mulching:** Replenish every 3-4 months
- **Pruning:** Remove dead/crossing branches annually
- **Pest monitoring:** Check monthly for infestations
- **Fertilizing:** Compost annually (December-January)
