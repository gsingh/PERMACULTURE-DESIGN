### **Essential Agreements (Put in Writing!):**

#### **Financial Agreement:**
- **Initial capital contribution:** Equal or proportional to house size?
- **Monthly contributions:** Shared expenses (utilities, maintenance, insurance)

- **Income distribution:** If healing center or farm produces income
- **Exit buy-out formula:** How to value someone's share if they leave
- **Inheritance:** What happens if family member passes away

#### **Decision-Making Protocol:**
- **Consensus required:** Major decisions (building, land use changes)
- **Majority vote:** Operational decisions (planting schedule, animal purchases)
- **Individual autonomy:** Private house interior, personal gardens
- **Meeting frequency:** Weekly check-ins, monthly planning, quarterly reviews
- **Facilitator rotation:** Different family leads each month
- **Conflict resolution:** Step 1: Direct conversation, Step 2: Group mediation, Step 3: External mediator

#### **Work Contribution Agreement:**
- **Shared labor expectations:** Hours per week each family contributes
- **Skill-based contributions:** Carpentry, gardening, cooking valued equally
- **Opt-out option:** Pay others to do your share if needed
- **Seasonal variations:** More work needed at harvest, planting
- **Age/ability considerations:** Adjusted expectations for elderly, disabled

#### **Lifestyle Agreements:**
- **Noise guidelines:** Quiet hours (e.g., 10pm-7am)
- **Guest policies:** Overnight visitors, long-term guests
- **Pet agreements:** Types, numbers, containment
- **Substance use:** Alcohol, smoking policies in common areas
- **Dietary norms:** Shared meals vegetarian? Organic only?
- **Children's boundaries:** Discipline approaches, screen time

### **Conflict Resolution Process:**

**Level 1: Direct Communication (24-48 hours)**
- Parties meet privately, attempt resolution
- Use "I feel" statements, active listening
- Goal: Understanding, not winning

**Level 2: Community Mediation (1 week)**
- Neutral community member facilitates
- Full group awareness if affects everyone
- Documented agreement on resolution

**Level 3: Professional Mediation (If Level 2 fails)**
- Hire external professional mediator
- Cost shared by parties in conflict
- Binding resolution agreement

**Level 4: Legal Action (Last Resort)**
- Partnership agreement specifies arbitration process
- Avoid if possibleâ€”destroys community trust
