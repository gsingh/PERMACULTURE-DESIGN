### Site-Specific Facts

**Location & Dimensions:**
- **Boundaries:** Swale 1 (north) to Swale 2 (south)
- **Dimensions:** 50 feet wide x 836 feet long
- **Total Area:** ~41,800 square feet (0.96 acres)
- **Shape:** Linear, running east-west along swales
- **Orientation:** Full north-south exposure (optimal sunlight)

**Climate Data (Kurukshetra):**
- **Summer:** 40-45Â°C temperatures (April-June), extreme heat stress for animals
- **Monsoon:** 450-600mm rainfall (July-September), ideal for tree establishment
- **Winter:** 5-15Â°C (December-February), comfortable for animals and humans
- **Growing season:** ~300 days (continuous food production possible)
- **Prevailing winds:** Hot westerly winds in summer (shade critical)

**Water Availability:**
- **Swale 1 (north edge):** Primary water capture, overflow infiltrates Zone 2
- **Swale 2 (south edge):** Secondary capture + pond inlet channel
- **Pond (SE corner):** 30,000-38,000 gallons, duck habitat + irrigation reserve
- **Tubewell access:** ~200 feet from Zone 2 center (backup water)
- **Rainwater harvesting:** Guest room contributes 15,000 liters/monsoon

**Soil Conditions:**
- **pH:** 7.5-8.5 (alkaline, common in Haryana)
- **Organic matter:** Low initially (1-2% from chemical farming legacy)
- **Texture:** Sandy loam (good drainage but low water retention)
- **Depth:** 3-5 feet before hardpan
- **Water retention:** Needs improvement (compost + mulching essential)

**Existing Features:**
- **Swale berms:** Trees already planted (from swale construction plan)
- **Access paths:** Along northern edge (from Zone 1) and central spine
- **Pond:** SE corner complete with inlet channel from Swale 2
- **Site clearing:** Invasive species removal needed (Lantana, Parthenium)

### Maintenance Requirements

**Daily Tasks (Animals):**
- **Time:** 30-45 minutes total
- **Tasks:** Egg collection (2x/day), feeding, watering, coop opening/closing
- **Who:** Rotating weekly among 4 families
- **Skill:** Low (training provided)

**Weekly Tasks (Food Forest):**
- **Time:** 2-3 hours total
- **Tasks:** Watering (if dry), mulching, weeding, pest monitoring
- **Who:** Rotating weekly among 4 families
- **Skill:** Moderate (pruning, plant identification)

**Monthly Tasks:**
- **Time:** 4-6 hours total
- **Tasks:** Deep coop cleaning, paddock rotation, compost turning, tree care
- **Who:** All 4 families together (community work day)
- **Skill:** Moderate to advanced

**Seasonal Intensity:**
- **High:** Monsoon planting season (June-July), harvest season (Year 3+)
- **Moderate:** Daily animal care year-round
- **Low:** Winter dormancy (December-February)
