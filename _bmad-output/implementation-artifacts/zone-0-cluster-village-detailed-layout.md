### Passive Solar Design Features (All Houses)

**South-Facing:**
- **Large windows:** 60-70% of south wall glazed
- **Thermal mass:** Concrete floor or earth blocks (stores heat)
- **Overhang:** 1.5-2 ft projection (blocks summer sun, allows winter sun)
- **Cross-ventilation:** North-south window placement

**North Side:**
- **Minimal windows:** 10-20% of north wall glazed (reduce heat loss)
- **Insulation:** Extra insulation on north wall (hotter summers buffer)
- **Utility spaces:** Bathroom, storage on north (thermal buffer)

**West Side:**
- **Minimal or no windows:** West sun is brutal in summer
- **Shaded:** Plant trees or vines on west side
- **Thick walls:** Extra thermal mass

**East Side:**
- **Small windows okay:** Morning sun is gentler
- **Kitchen on east:** Natural light for cooking

**Roof:**
- **Reflective color:** White or light colors (reduce heat gain)
- **Insulation:** Min 6 inches (R-19 or better)
- **Ventilation:** Ridge vent + soffit vents (hot air escape)

**Cooling Strategies:**
- **Deep porch:** 6-10 ft creates outdoor living space
- **Ceiling fans:** Every room (reduces perceived temp 4-5Â°C)
- **Natural ventilation:** High windows + low windows create airflow
- **Deciduous vines:** On south porch (shade summer, sun winter)
