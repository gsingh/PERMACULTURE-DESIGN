## 4-Family Permaculture Farm, Kurukshetra, Haryana

**Date:** January 2026  
**Property:** 4 acres, 4-family cluster village  
**Grid Status:** Rural connection (frequent outages expected)  
**Climate:** Semi-arid (excellent solar potential)

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Rural Haryana experiences **frequent power outages** (2-8 hours daily, especially summer peaks). For a permaculture farm, reliable electricity is critical for:

**Essential Power Needs:**
1. **Fire suppression pumps** (emergency priority)
2. **Irrigation pumps** (crop survival)
3. **Water supply pumps** (household needs)
4. **Cold storage/refrigeration** (preserve produce)
5. **Communication** (phones, internet during emergencies)
6. **Lighting** (security and convenience)
7. **Tools/equipment** (processing, workshops)

**Recommended Solution:** **Hybrid Solar + Battery + Diesel Backup**
- Primary: Solar PV system (clean, abundant in Kurukshetra)
- Secondary: Battery storage (3-5 hours backup)
- Tertiary: Diesel generator (emergency/extended outages)

**Total Investment:** ‚Çπ4.5-8.5 lakhs (~$5,400-10,200 USD) for complete system
**Payback Period:** 4-6 years (vs. diesel-only operation)
**Lifespan:** 20-25 years (solar panels), 8-10 years (batteries)

## üî• FIRE SAFETY INTEGRATION

### Emergency Power for Fire Pumps (CRITICAL)

**Requirements:**
- **Response time:** <30 seconds from power loss to pump start
- **Runtime:** Minimum 2 hours continuous at full load
- **Reliability:** 99.9% availability during fire season

**System Design:**

```
FIRE PUMP POWER PRIORITY CASCADE:

LEVEL 1: Grid Power (Normal operation)
  ‚îî‚îÄ Pump runs on grid electricity when available
     ‚Üì (if grid fails)

LEVEL 2: Battery + Inverter (Immediate backup)
  ‚îî‚îÄ Inverter switches to battery in <0.1 seconds
     ‚îî‚îÄ Fire pump continues running (no interruption)
     ‚îî‚îÄ Battery provides 2-4 hours runtime (depending on bank size)
     ‚Üì (if battery depletes OR need extended operation)

LEVEL 3: Diesel Generator (Extended backup)
  ‚îî‚îÄ Manual or automatic start (1-2 minutes)
     ‚îî‚îÄ Generator takes over load from battery
     ‚îî‚îÄ Provides unlimited runtime (with fuel)
     ‚îî‚îÄ Battery recharges while generator runs
     ‚Üì (if generator fails - unlikely)

LEVEL 4: Portable Generator / Manual Operation
  ‚îî‚îÄ Portable generator manually connected
     OR
  ‚îî‚îÄ Manual fire suppression (buckets, beaters)
```

**Dedicated Fire Pump Circuit:**
- Separate dedicated circuit breaker
- Priority switching (fire pump never disconnected)
- Emergency override switch (manual control)
- Visual/audible alarm if fire pump circuit loses power
- Battery reserve always maintained at >50% during fire season
