- Pond at 770-820ft from north boundary (southern end, in Zone 5 wilderness area)

## üåø ZONE 5 SEASONAL WETLAND - True Wilderness

### Location & Specifications:

**Location:** 700-836ft from north boundary (southern 136 feet of property)

**Size:** 
- **Area:** 0.65 acres (28,424 sq ft) = 16% of farm
- **Wetland portion:** Variable (nature determines based on swale overflow)

**Formation Method:** NATURAL ONLY - NOT excavated

### What Happens:

**During Heavy Monsoon:**
- Final swales (Swale 13-14 @ 710-760ft) overflow
- Water flows into Zone 5, pools in low spots naturally
- Depth: 6 inches to 3 feet (shallow, determined by topography)
- Size: Variable year to year (depends on monsoon intensity)

**During Dry Season:**
- Wetland may dry partially or completely (this is natural seasonal rhythm)
- Some depressions may hold water year-round (if clay layer present)
- Native plants adapted to seasonal flooding survive

**Over Years (Natural Succession):**
- Year 1-3: Wetland grasses establish (volunteer species)
- Year 3-5: Cattails, sedges, reeds colonize naturally
- Year 5-10: Wetland shrubs establish on edges
- Year 10+: Mature wetland ecosystem with riparian trees

### Wildlife Benefits:

**Amphibians:**
- Frogs, toads breed in seasonal pools (eggs laid in monsoon)
- Tadpoles develop in 2-3 months before dry season
- Adults disperse to Zones 1-4 (eat slugs, insects, mosquitoes)

**Insects:**
- Dragonflies, damselflies breed (nymphs aquatic, adults control mosquitoes)
- Water beetles, water striders colonize
- Pollinators use wetland edges (native wildflowers)

**Birds:**
- Water birds visit during monsoon (herons, egrets, kingfishers)
- Seed-eating birds year-round (wetland grass seeds)
- Nesting habitat in surrounding Zone 5 forest

**Value to Farm:**
- ‚úÖ Pollinator reservoir spills into Zones 1-4 (10-20% yield increase)
- ‚úÖ Pest predators (frogs, dragonflies, birds) reduce pest pressure
- ‚úÖ Biodiversity hotspot (genetic insurance for climate resilience)
- ‚úÖ Educational lab (observe natural succession, seasonal dynamics)

### Management Rules (100% Hands-Off):

**ALLOWED:**
- ‚ùå NO excavation (let nature determine depth/size)
- ‚ùå NO planting wetland species (they volunteer naturally)
- ‚ùå NO clearing vegetation around wetland
- ‚ùå NO accessing wetland for irrigation (this is wildlife's water)
- ‚ùå NO introducing fish or ducks to Zone 5 wetland
- ‚ùå NO harvesting plants, frogs, or any organisms

**ONLY allowed:**
- ‚úÖ Observe from distance (10+ meters from wetland edge)
- ‚úÖ Photograph/document (without disturbing wildlife)
- ‚úÖ Educate children (from observation posts outside Zone 5)

### Cost (Zone 5 Wetland):

**Construction:** ‚Çπ0 (no excavation, no management)
**Annual maintenance:** ‚Çπ0 (zero intervention)

**Investment required:** ‚Çπ0 per family

**Ecosystem service value:** ‚Çπ3,750-10,000 per family per year (pollination, pest control, biodiversity)

**ROI:** INFINITE (no cost, high benefit)
