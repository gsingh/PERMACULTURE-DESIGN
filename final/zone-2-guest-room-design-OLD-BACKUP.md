## 4. GUEST EXPERIENCE DESIGN

### "Living Classroom" Concept

**Upon Arrival (Afternoon/Evening):**
1. **Welcome walk:** Host walks guests 500 ft through food forest (10-15 min)

   - Veranda sitting (sunset through trees)
   - Sound of ducks returning from pond (if evening)

   - 110-130 ft forest walk
   - See ducks swimming, fish activity

   - Return for breakfast

   - Farm tour (Zone 1 gardens, Zone 0 houses, animals)
