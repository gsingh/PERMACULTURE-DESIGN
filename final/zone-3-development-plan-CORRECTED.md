### Orchard Management

**Year 1-3 (Establishment):**

**Planting (June-July Monsoon Timing):**
- [ ] Dig planting pits: 2 ft Ã— 2 ft Ã— 2 ft (larger trees: 3 ft Ã— 3 ft Ã— 3 ft)
- [ ] Amend soil:
  - 50% topsoil
  - 30% compost/manure
  - 10-15% sand (if clay soil)
  - Neem cake: 100-200 grams/pit (pest deterrent)
  - Rock phosphate: 50 grams/pit (root growth)
- [ ] Plant saplings at correct depth (root collar at soil level)
- [ ] Create water basins (6-inch berm, 3-4 ft diameter)
- [ ] Apply 4-6 inch mulch layer (keep 3-4 inches from trunk)
- [ ] Install drip irrigation (2-4 emitters per tree)

**Watering Schedule:**
- **Monsoon (July-Sept):** Daily first 2 weeks, then every 2-3 days, then weekly if no rain
- **Post-monsoon (Oct-Dec):** Twice weekly (15-20 L/tree)
- **Winter (Jan-Feb):** Weekly (10-15 L/tree)
- **Summer (March-June):** 3x weekly (20-30 L/tree) - CRITICAL for survival

**Tree Protection:**
- [ ] Wire guards (3-ft diameter) around each tree (protect from animals)
- [ ] Stake trees (if needed in first year)
- [ ] Regular mulch replenishment (maintain 4-6 inch depth)

**Pest & Disease Monitoring:**
- [ ] Weekly observation walk
- [ ] Neem oil spray: Every 2 weeks (preventative)
- [ ] Physical removal: Hand-pick large pests
- [ ] Beneficial insects: Plant marigold, fennel between trees

**Fertilization:**
- **Month 8 (Post-monsoon):** 5-10 kg compost/tree
- **Month 11 (Pre-winter):** Vermicompost 3-5 kg/tree + Neem cake 200g
- **Ongoing:** Collect animal manure from Zone 2, compost 2-3 months, apply

**Pruning (Year 2-3):**
- **Formative pruning:** Establish 3-5 main scaffold branches
- **Remove:** Crossing/rubbing branches, branches <18 inches from ground
- **Timing:** Winter (December-January) when trees dormant
- **Technique:** Clean cuts, 45Â° angle above bud
