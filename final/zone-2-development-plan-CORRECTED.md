### Maintenance Requirements

**Daily Tasks (Animals - 30-45 minutes):**
- Morning (6-7 AM): Open coops, check headcount, refill water
- Egg collection: Check nesting boxes (chickens 2x/day, ducks 1x/day)
- Evening (6-7 PM): Close coops, headcount, lock securely
- **Rotating responsibility:** 1 family per week (fair distribution)

**Weekly Tasks (Food Forest - 2-3 hours):**
- Watering check: Soil moisture test (finger 4 inches deep)
- Drip irrigation: Adjust timers if needed
- Mulching: Replenish around young trees
- Weeding: 3-foot radius around saplings (Year 1-3)
- Pest monitoring: Visual check for insects, diseases
- **Shared work:** Community Saturday (all families 1-2 hours)

**Monthly Tasks (4-6 hours):**
- Coop/house deep clean: Remove all bedding, scrub, fresh litter
- Paddock rotation: Move fencing (if using portable system)
- Compost turning: 3-bay system maintenance
- Tree care: Prune if needed, check guards, apply compost
- Health check: Inspect each animal closely
- **All families together:** Monthly work day

**Seasonal Intensity:**
- **High:** Monsoon tree planting (June-July), harvest season (Year 3+)
- **Moderate:** Daily animal care year-round
- **Low:** Winter dormancy (tree growth slows, animal care continues)
